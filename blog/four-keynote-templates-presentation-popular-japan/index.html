4 Popular Keynote Templates in Japan

This article shows some Keynote templates which are popular in Japanese.



Azusa Colors



Azusa Colors Template is one of the most popular template among Japanese engineers.
Many engineers includes me use this fashionable template!

Zebra



Zebra was made for researcher’s presentation.
There are no exotic decoration in the template, so it is easy to read for audience.

Mint Green, Violent, Scarlet



Mint Green, Violent, Scarlet are fancy and feminine.
Them are so useful and uniq Keynote templates.

Lemonade



Lemonade is unique template too.
If you have a plan to use big image in your presentation, I think that this template is good selection.
